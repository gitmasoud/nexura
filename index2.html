02 — Recent Work

Enterprise Change Process

Automating change strategies for a global organisation.

We were approached to prototype a tool that automates change strategy and recommendations. The problem was simple: post-meeting, change strategies are tiresome to produce, contain errors, and surface clashing ideas between stakeholders.

The client wanted change meetings to be more streamlined — and strategies easier to produce with fewer errors.

The solution: a system that ingests change documents (structured and unstructured), runs an agentic workflow against an open-source LLM to understand company and change context, then produces a strategy deck that gets passed to every key stakeholder.
